# Watch List Management

## Use Case Summary
Use Recorded Future Watch Lists to maintain curated, high-priority entities (companies, domains, IPs, vulnerabilities, executives, suppliers) so alerts and intelligence focus on what matters most to the Security Operations Center (SOC).

For further information on Watch lists click [here](https://support.recordedfuture.com/hc/en-us/articles/115005092427-Watch-Lists).

## Issue
Threat environments and business priorities change constantly. Without actively maintained Watch Lists, alerts and intelligence may be too broad, making it harder for analysts to focus quickly on what matters most.

## Solution
Create and maintain Watch Lists directly in PS Banshee using [`banshee list`](../../reference/commands/#banshee-list) commands. Upload CSVs for bulk changes, add entities individually, or request curation for new ones. Map Watch Lists to Intelligence Goals so only relevant alerts are triggered. Review and refresh the lists regularly (brands, domains, executives, suppliers, vulnerabilities, locations, etc.) to ensure coverage reflects your evolving risk landscape.
